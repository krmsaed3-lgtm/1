How does registration work?›
Sign up with your phone number and an invitation code. Your account is created securely and linked to your inviter.
How does IPower work?›
You can complete one IPower action per day. Earnings are calculated automatically and credited to your wallet.
What is the difference between balance and reserved?›
Available balance can be used immediately. Reserved balance is temporarily locked during review or processing.
